# Energy

[Energy](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/energy) is a property of matter and space.

- Can be transferred between objects
- Cannot be crated or destroy
- Can be used to heat, move or illuminate

## Measures

$$1J = 1Ws = 1Nm = 1kg\cdot m^2/s^{-2}$$

### [Joule](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/joule)

- Unit of energy

### [Watt](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/watt)

- Unit of power

**Some approximations**
- 1 kWh = 3 day of hiking
- 10kWh = 1l of diesel

### [Newton](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/newton_(unit))

- Unit of force
- Measure how much force is required to make 1kg accelerate 1m/s per second (1N=1kg·m/s²)
- On Earth, a mass of 1kg pushes on its support with an average force of 9.8N

## Type of energy

- [Chemical energy](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/chemical_energy "chemical energy")
- [Sound energy](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/sound_energy "sound energy")
- [Renewable energy](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/renewable_energy "renewable energy")
- [Solar energy](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/solar_energy "solar energy")
- [Nuclear energy](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/nuclear_energy "nuclear energy")
- [Elastic energy](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/elastic_energy "elastic energy")
- [Gravitational potential energy](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/gravitational_energy "gravitational energy")
- [Kinetic energy](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/kinetic_energy "kinetic energy")
- [Dark energy](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/dark_energy "dark energy")
- [Hamiltonian mechanics](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/hamiltonian_mechanics "hamiltonian mechanics")
- [Internal energy](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/internal_energy "internal energy")

For example:

1. human throws the stone using energy stored in muscles = [chemical energy](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/chemical_energy "chemical energy")
2. stone moves upwards = [kinetic energy](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/kinetic_energy "kinetic energy")
3. stone at the highest point = [potential energy](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/potential_energy "potential energy")
4. stone falls to ground = kinetic energy
5. stone hits ground = [thermal energy](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/thermal_energy "thermal energy")/[sonic energy](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/sonic_energy "sonic energy")
